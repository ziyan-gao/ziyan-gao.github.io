## ðŸ“‹ Overview

This work proposes a novel framework that integrates packing policy with structural stability validation and heuristic planning to overcome limitations in online 3D bin packing. The method introduces **Load Bearable Convex Polygon (LBCP)** for computationally efficient stable loading positions, and **Stable Rearrangement Planning (SRP)** for safely reconfiguring the bin when new items cannot be placed directly.

<div class="section-box">
  <h3>Key Contributions</h3>
  <ul class="features">
    <li>Load Bearable Convex Polygon (LBCP) for fast stability validation without complex physics simulation</li>
    <li>Actor-critic packing policy framework integrated with stability constraints</li>
    <li>Stable Rearrangement Planning (SRP) module for efficient item rearrangement</li>
    <li>Real-world validation on 6-DOF robotic platform with RGB-D sensing</li>
    <li>Demonstrated efficiency on benchmark datasets and real robotic systems</li>
  </ul>
</div>

## ðŸŽ¨ Framework Architecture

![Framework Overview](https://ziyan-gao.github.io/images/2025-07-12-online-3d-bin-packing.png)

<div class="highlight-box">
  <strong>Figure 1: System Architecture</strong><br>
  The proposed framework consists of three key modules: <strong>Structural Stability Validation</strong>, <strong>Packing Policy</strong>, and <strong>Safe Rearrangement Planning</strong>. Stability is ensured by the structural stability validation process, which identifies unstable placements based on the feasibility map. This validation is seamlessly integrated into both the packing policy and safe rearrangement planning modules to enforce stability constraints throughout. The packing policy module adopts an actorâ€“critic framework, where the actor selects a loading position from candidates generated by the placement module, and the critic network estimates the expected bin utilization. If no stable placement is found, the safe rearrangement planning module generates a sequence of rearrangement actions on packed items to create space for an incoming item.
</div>
